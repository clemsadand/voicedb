👋 Welcome to VoiceDB Assistant

This assistant lets you control a database using voice or text. You will be interacting with a dummy SQL database.

🎯 Supported Actions

  • 📖 Read: read or read row 5
  • ✏️ Update: update row 4 set color to red
  • ➕ Create: add a red chair in furniture with price 99 and quantity 10
  • ❌ Delete: delete row 8
  • 🔍 Filter: show all electronics where quantity > 10
  • ↕️ Sort: sort by price or sort by quantity
  • duplicate row 3
  • 📊 Stats: show statistics, show KPIs, or summarize inventory

💡 What You Can Say:

  • Update the quantity of row 3 to 15
  • Delete row 5
  • Filter products where price > 100
  • Give me stats

🎤 How to Use:

  1. Click the microphone to record your voice.
  2. Wait for it to transcribe your intent.
  3. Validate the transcription.
  4. The system will interpret and execute your command.

Tip: Try speaking naturally. If the system doesn’t understand, try being more specific. You can always type too!
